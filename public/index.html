Destinations

Explore four unique regions, each with its own sights and experiences.

Chiang Mai

Nestled in northern mountains, this city charms with ancient temples, vibrant night markets, and serene countryside.

Bangkok

Thailand’s bustling capital, full of grand palaces, colorful markets, and vibrant street life.

Phuket

Sun-soaked beaches and crystal-clear waters perfect for relaxation and adventure.

Ayutthaya

A historic city of ancient ruins and temples, offering a glimpse into Thailand’s rich past.
